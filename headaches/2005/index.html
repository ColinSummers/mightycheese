2005

Fewer than I would have expected. I might not have been keeping careful track, although I was already writing to Dean when I took a Vicodin, so I should have an entry for each headache.

  • tue nov 8 2005
    To Nell in mail:

    I am sorry to have a headache on your birthday. I took a vicodin when a shower, some quiet, and reading with Dexter did nothing to slow down the migraine. By 9pm I should be fine, but a little loopy. I would love to watch something with you.
  • tue feb 1 2005

    To Dean in Gmail:

    A three hour meeting in the Valley with a client on a project that we should have been fired from months ago. I knew on the way home that I was going to have a migraine. I might have had it by the time I made it off the 405. Took a Vicodin and ended my day a little early.

  • wed jan 12 2005
    To Dean in Gmail:
     I am back from meetings in Vegas. I thought napping on the plane wouldhelp. My tongue is trembling and my skull is starting to squeeze my brain like a sponge.

    Captain V to the rescue.
