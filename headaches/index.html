Headaches

For a long time I have ignored the frequency of my migraines and figured that each one that I had would just be the last one. Starting tonight (August 2006) I will keep a log of them, so that as I try to find a solution I will have a record to work from. I have learned some things, which is good, but I need to get better. They are bad to have and I need to figure out how to manage or avoid them entirely.

I will work on a history page, because I have had them since I was twelve or thirteen. I had them regularly in college, then frequently soon after college, and then I had an eight year break from them when Nell and I first got together. Fairly recently, they returned. 

Stupidly, it shows them in alphabetically order. Ah well. If you click on the year you get a page with the entries in the correct order (most recent at top, like a blog). Here they are sorted by day, which is something.

  • 2007

    This is meant to be the year without migraines. Even if I have to be taking handfuls of Vicodin, meditating in a trance for a few hours a day and firing clients left and right, I am going to try to not have a single bad headache.
