tue nov 8 2005

To Nell in mail:

I am sorry to have a headache on your birthday. I took a vicodin when a shower, some quiet, and reading with Dexter did nothing to slow down the migraine. By 9pm I should be fine, but a little loopy. I would love to watch something with you.

Part of my stress is a feeling of fucking this up. I like working hard, getting paid for my time, and seeing the result. I don't like the nebulous "you've left money on the table" or "you've scared them off" feelings I get from this deal. [I was selling tightcircle at the time.]
