pog | FHR Condo

Unit F212 at the Cannery Condo Village is about 1,110 square feet. Two bedrooms, two bathrooms. WiFi (eero network) spraying FiOS broadband. Old flat screen television fed by an AppleTV unit, next to the recumbent bike. Microwave, toaster oven, electric range and oven.

The condo is in the F building of the Village, overlooking the ferry parking and, at the narrow end, out over the ferry landing itself. It is a really short walk to almost anything in town. Kings is the grocery store. We tend to eat at Cafe Demeter in the morning, Market Chef for lunch, and either Cask & Schooner or Downriggers for dinners.

The place is simply furnished, mostly IKEA. The master bedroom has the bath en suite and the guest room has it across the hall (so it also works as a powder room). There is a little Chevy Spark EV parked in an assigned spot one flight up from the front door.

It is not luxurious, but it is cozy, with a nice wood stove insert in the fireplace and views of the harbor.
