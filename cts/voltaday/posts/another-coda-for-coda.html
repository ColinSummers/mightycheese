cts | Volt a Day | Another Coda for Coda

November 11, 2010

I'm sure there's a musical term for that. But Wired ran an article on Monday that sure wasn't good news for the company. Note: when you are about to launch your flagship product, do not have the CEO step down, no matter what the story is that you offer the public. No one wants to buy that car now.

Maybe they never were meant to. Maybe Coda is some sort of money laundering operation that I don't understand. A very clever man deep in Washington politics once explained to me that one of the perennial presidential candidates ran three times because it made a lot of money for his sister (the campaign advisor) and him (the candidate). It all had to do with matching funds and a cynicism that I can't muster even on my worst days.

Anyone have their eye on a Coda?
