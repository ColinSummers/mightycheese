October 25, 2010

It doesn't look like the Volt is going to get here this week. That's a bit discouraging.

I need to report on the PlugIn America dinner, which was a lot of fun (and another opportunity to sit in the Volt). And on the charger installation (an epic which continues because Santa Monica's electrical inspector wouldn't sign off on the wiring performed).

And I had to restore my post about Lyle Dennis because he refused to discuss how the community might be helped with a few simple ground rules. Or, more to the point, he refused to discuss it unless I agreed it would all be strictly private, that I couldn't report to my readers what he had to say about censoring the community, deleting threads, editing posts or discouraging discourse. I'll post his emails to me on the subject, incorrectly characterizing my post as slander and demanding I not quote him, when I get a moment.

For now I will continue to read cool reports from the Volt Unplugged tour on Voltage and wish that one of the cars had been left in Los Angeles for me.
