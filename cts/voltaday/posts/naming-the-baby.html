cts | Volt a Day | Naming the Baby November 14, 2010

When you fire up the cool iPhone application for your OnStar account it prompts you to name your GM vehicle. My Volt is named Alessandro, since I am a geek.

That's sort of obvious. Alessandro Volta lending his name to your Volt. It would be cool if after the first three months of shipping cars GM did a baby-naming chart and showed what the most common names were. I imagine a lot of people will just let the default "My Volt" work for them.

But it's a high-tech vehicle and a lot are going to do a cool, historic name, right? Volta himself?

And if there were the same sort of application for the Tesla, how many are naming their cars Nikola?

As I write this, Alessandro is 77% charged and awaiting the rate-drop at midnight to start charging. According to the iPhone.
