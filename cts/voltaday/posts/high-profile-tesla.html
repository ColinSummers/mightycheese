cts | Volt a Day | High Profile Tesla September 21, 2010

Keep an eye on Wired.com  My copy of the magazine arrived in the mail and the cover story about Elon Musk and the Tesla Company is fascinating. He came so close to having the whole thing collapsing, but Daimler and Toyoda (the CEO of Toyota) saved him in the end.

The only article I could find online today was about Elon running out of cash. I love an entrepreneur willing to bet his fortune on what he believes in. I wish there were more CEOs at big companies in the United States that were willing to do that.

And yes, I would like to have a Roadster 2.5, but the car really doesn't make any sense for our family. I guess once the kids leave for college. Or maybe once they have finished high school I can use their college funds to buy a Tesla instead. (The Model S is meant to seat seven, and apparently there is a set of seats that face backwards. Who is the target market for that? Not my family, that's for sure.)
